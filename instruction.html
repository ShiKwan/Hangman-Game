Suicidal Chocobo

(Final Fantasy Edition!)

Save chocobo by guessing the correct character within 10 tries

Tips for Instructor/TA:

For grading purpose: please play in "Fast Pace" mode. Check console for character's name!

Game Play Instruction

This game has three different mode.

  • Easy - 20 tries, 10 life
  • Intermediate - 12 tries, 10 life (DEFAULT)
  • Fast Pace - 10 tries, 10 life

Easy and Intermediate

  • If you guess the character correctly or used up all your guess chances, game will show character info after each guess.
  • Chocobo warks if you guessed a correct letter.
  • Chocobo (that yellow bird) will moves closer to cliff after each wrong guess and fell down the cliff and you lose a life.
  • After each guess, hit "y" to start the next character, "n" to quit game.

Fast Pace

  • Simple and clean, no chocobo, character introduction b.s.
Navigation menu: - Click on mode to change game play mode - Click the audio hyperlink to toggle on/off background music. - Click on Game to go back to game.
